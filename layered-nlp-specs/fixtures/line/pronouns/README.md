# Pronouns

Description: Pronoun coreference (linking a pronoun to its antecedent = earlier entity).

References:
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pronoun
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coreference

Examples:
- "The Tenant agrees. It shall pay."
- "The Parties agree they will comply."

Edge cases:
- "this" as a relative vs a deictic reference.

Difficulty:
- Ambiguous antecedents, number agreement, and distance across sentences.
