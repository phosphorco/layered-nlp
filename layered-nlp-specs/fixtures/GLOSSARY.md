# Glossary

Deontic modality: language of duties, permissions, or prohibitions (shall, must, may).
Coreference: linking a mention (name or pronoun) to an earlier entity.
Antecedent: the earlier entity a pronoun or reference points to.
Modal verb: an auxiliary verb expressing modality (shall, must, may, can, will).
Semantic roles: who does what to whom (agent, patient, beneficiary).
Scope: the text span a condition or negation applies to.
