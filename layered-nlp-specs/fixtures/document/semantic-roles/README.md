# Semantic Roles

Description: Who does what to whom (semantic role labeling = mapping arguments to roles).

References:
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semantic_role_labeling
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thematic_relation

Examples:
- "The Buyer shall pay the Seller" (agent Buyer, beneficiary Seller).
- "The fee shall be paid by the Buyer" (passive voice).

Edge cases:
- Implicit agents.

Difficulty:
- Handling passive voice, implicit actors, and prepositional phrase attachment.
